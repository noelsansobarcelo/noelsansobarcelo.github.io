LOL Surprise! B.B.s BORN TO TRAVEL - Unity C#



L.O.L. Surprise! is a dolls game developed for all consoles where you can choose your character, collect fabulous L.O.L. Surprise! dolls full of glitter, diamonds and more, and team up with up to four friends!

In this project I contributed as a gameplay developer to the cooking mini-games where you have to take and prepare all kind of orders.

The main features that I contributed to include:

  • Character controller.
  • Camera controller using Cinmeachine.
  • Interactables system.
  • Recipe generator using Scriptable Objects.
